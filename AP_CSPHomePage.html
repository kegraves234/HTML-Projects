    Unit 3: Into to Appdesign ▼

    Lesson 1: Introduction to Apps ▼

    Question of the Day: What makes an app an app?

    Students explore and investigate what makes an app an app by reviewing five different apps, watching a video about how computers work, and analyzing inputs and outputs.

    Download Lesson Plan (PDF)
    Lesson 2: Introduction to Design Mode ▼

    Question of the Day: How do we use Design Mode to layout an app?

    Students progress through levels to learn Design Mode and practice replicating app layouts by copying an image of an app interface.

    Download Lesson Plan (PDF)
    Lesson 3: Project - Designing an App Part 1 ▼

    Question of the Day: How do we plan the design of an app?

    Students brainstorm app ideas, sketch user interfaces, and begin transferring designs into App Lab for their unit project.

    Download Lesson Plan (PDF)
    Lesson 4: The Need for Programming Languages ▼

    Question of the Day: Why do we need programming languages?

    Students explore the challenges of communicating instructions clearly and the need for programming languages to remove ambiguities.

    Download Lesson Plan (PDF)
    Lesson 5: Intro to Programming ▼

    Question of the Day: How do programs respond to user interactions?

    Students use and modify simple apps to explore programming commands and observe how code runs, including event-driven programming.

    Download Lesson Plan (PDF)
    Lesson 6: Debugging ▼

    Question of the Day: What is debugging, and why is it important?

    Students practice debugging programming errors and reflect on how the debugging process helps them improve their understanding of coding concepts.

    Download Lesson Plan (PDF)
    Lesson 7: Project - Designing an App Part 2 ▼

    Question of the Day: How do we add functionality to our apps?

    Students work on coding their apps, receive feedback, and make final adjustments. They complete their projects by testing and refining based on peer reviews.

    Download Lesson Plan (PDF)
    Lesson 8: Assessment Day ▼

    Question of the Day: How well do we understand app design?

    Students complete the unit assessment and spend the remaining class time sharing and discussing their projects.
